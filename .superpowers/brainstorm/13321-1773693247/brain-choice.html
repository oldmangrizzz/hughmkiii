How should H.U.G.H. (v2.0) process information?

Option 1: The Unified Brain
Integrated Approach

MemGPT and Cognee share the same data foundation.

  • Pros: More "intuitive" jumps between topics; deeper context.
  • Cons: Higher complexity; harder to debug "hallucinated" connections.
